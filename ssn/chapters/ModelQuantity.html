Quantity Values and Units of Measure

The result of an sosa:Observation or an sosa:Actuation can be a quantity value with a numeric value and a unit of measurement.

It is out of the scope of this specification to recommend a particular way of representing quantitative results. Several external vocabularies are available for expressing quantities as OWL individuals, or as datatypes. Examples include the Quantities, Units, Dimensions and Data Types Ontologies [[QUDT]] , and the St Etienne School of Mines Custom Datatypes [[CDT]].
