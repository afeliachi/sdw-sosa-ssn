Dolce-Ultralite Alignment Module

This section introduces the alignment of SSN to the DOLCE UltraLite upper ontology (DUL) which is the core dependency of the previous version of SSN. This serves to axiomatically clarify the intended meaning of SSN terms and will assist SSN users wishing to interoperate with other DUL-aligned ontologies. Note, however, that the DUL alignment can be used independently to align SSN with more generic concepts/properties of DUL.

The DUL alignment, known as "ssn-dul" is available at http://www.w3.org/ns/ssn/dul.

This section uses [[Description Logics]] notations (see also the [[Description Logics Home Page]]).
⊑ denotes either SubClassOf or SubObjectPropertyOf, depending on the context.

Namespaces

The following namespace prefixes are used in the alignment to SOSA and SSN

sosa:
http://www.w3.org/ns/sosa/
dul:
http://www.ontologydesignpatterns.org/ont/dul/DUL.owl

Class Alignments

The following classes in SOSA and SSN can be aligned via a subclass relation as follows.

  • sosa:FeatureOfInterest ⊑ dul:Entity
  • sosa:Procedure ⊑ dul:Method
  • sosa:System ⊑ dul:Object
  • sosa:Actuator ⊑ dul:Object
  • sosa:Sensor ⊑ dul:Object
  • sosa:Sampler ⊑ dul:Object
  • sosa:Execution ⊑ dul:Event
  • sosa:Actuation ⊑ dul:Event
  • sosa:Observation ⊑ dul:Event
  • sosa:Sampling ⊑ dul:Event
  • sosa:Property ⊑ dul:Quality
  • sosa:Stimulus ⊑ dul:Event
  • sosa:Platform ⊑ dul:Object
  • sosa:Deployment ⊑ dul:Event

For more complex alignments, the axiomatic alignments are defined as follows.

  • (not (oldsosa:Observation)) and (old-sosa:observationResult some owl:Thing) ⊑ sosa:Observation

Property Alignments

Additional alignments from SOSA/SSN to DUL properties are defined as follows.

  • sosa:hasProperty ⊑ dul:hasQuality
  • sosa:isPropertyOf ⊑ dul:isQualityOf
  • sosa:hosts ⊑ dul:isLocationOf
  • sosa:isHostedBy ⊑ dul:hasLocation
  • sosa:implementedBy ⊑ dul:describes
  • sosa:implements ⊑ dul:isDescribedBy
  • sosa:usedProcedure ⊑ dul:hasParticipant
  • sosa:madeActuation ⊑ dul:associatedWith
  • sosa:madeObservation ⊑ dul:associatedWith
  • sosa:madeSampling ⊑ dul:associatedWith
  • sosa:madeByActuator ⊑ dul:associatedWith
  • sosa:madeBySensor ⊑ dul:associatedWith
  • sosa:madeBySampler ⊑ dul:associatedWith
  • sosa:actsOnProperty ⊑ dul:associatedWith
  • sosa:observedProperty ⊑ dul:associatedWith
  • sosa:hasResult ⊑ dul:associatedWith
  • sosa:phenomenonTime ⊑ dul:hasRegion
  • sosa:hasSubSystem ⊑ dul:hasPart
  • sosa:deployedOnPlatform ⊑ dul:associatedWith
  • sosa:deployedSystem ⊑ dul:associatedWith
  • sosa:hasDeployment ⊑ dul:associatedWith
  • sosa:inDeployment ⊑ dul:associatedWith
