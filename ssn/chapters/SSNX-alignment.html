SSNX Alignment Module

An alignment of the SSN Ontology to the version of SSN [[SSNX]] that was published by the original W3C Semantic Sensor Network Incubator Group is provided in the previous edition of this recommendation https://www.w3.org/TR/2017/REC-vocab-ssn-20171019/#SSNX_Alignment

The RDF representation of the alignment remains available at http://www.w3.org/2017/01/ssn-ssnx/.
