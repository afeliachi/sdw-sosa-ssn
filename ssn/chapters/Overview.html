Scope and overview of the ontology

The SSN Ontology provides a semantic framework for describing certain procedure-executions, covering actuations, observations, and samplings, the procedures executed, and the systems that implement the procedures. Supporting concepts include platforms, deployments, the features of interest and the observed or actuated properties, and resulting samples.

Classes for collections (sets) of executions and results are provided, since they are typically used for analysis and implementation.
